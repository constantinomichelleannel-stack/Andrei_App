# Cruz vs. Republic
G.R. No. 210987

This document represents the case of Cruz vs. Republic regarding the petition for cancellation of a certificate of live birth. The Supreme Court ruled on the necessity of adversarial proceedings for substantial changes in the civil registry.

### Citation Analysis
**Status:** VALID

**Details:** The citations in this case, particularly those referring to Rule 108 of the Rules of Court and the doctrine on adversarial proceedings for substantial corrections in the civil registry, remain valid and are consistently upheld in Philippine jurisprudence. Key precedents such as Republic vs. Valencia (141 SCRA 462) are correctly applied and remain good law.
